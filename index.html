Meet the Founder

Hillary Elia

Contractor Educator Mentor Community Builder Gospel Artist

With over 6+ years of hands-on experience in flooring, carpentry, painting, plumbing assistance, window installation, and full-scale renovations, Hillary Elia created DoItForJesus Academy to bridge the gap between faith, skill, and opportunity.

What started as a vision to teach young people how to work with their hands has grown into a life-changing program focused on discipline, purpose, and real-world success.

"We're going to teach them for real — not theory, not shortcuts. Real skills that build real futures."

The Curriculum

8 Weeks. Real Skills.
Life-Changing Results.

Small groups, hands-on instruction, and real-world application across multiple trades — plus the entrepreneurship mindset to own your future.

Week 01

Introduction to Construction

  • Tool identification & safety
  • Mindset of a builder
  • Foundations of the trades
Week 02

Framing

  • Basic structure building
  • Measuring, cutting & assembling
  • Real-world framing application
Week 03

Electrical

  • Wiring basics
  • Installing switches, outlets & lighting
  • Hands-on electrical builds
Week 04

HVAC

  • Understanding systems & airflow
  • Refrigerants and function
  • Career pathways in HVAC
Week 05

Plumbing

  • Pipe systems & layout
  • Soldering water lines
  • Understanding waste systems
Week 06

Interior Trades

  • Drywall installation
  • Flooring basics
  • Finishing work
Week 07

Exterior Trades

  • Siding, decking & structure
  • Outdoor construction concepts
  • Real-world building applications
Week 08

Business & Entrepreneurship

  • Entrepreneurship in construction
  • Customer service & communication
  • Marketing, branding & ownership
